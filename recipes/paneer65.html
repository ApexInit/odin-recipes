Paneer 65

Description

Paneer 65 is a dry starter or appetizer perfect for parties. Paneer 65 can be made using paneer marinated in spices mix, then dipped in a flour batter and deep fried until crisp. Paneer 65 can be either deep fried or shallow fried or baked or even air fried.

Paneer 65 is a vegetarian alternate to Chicken 65. Each 65 version have their origin from each region like Madurai, Chennai, Coimbatore, Bangalore style but this paneer 65 recipe is unique in its own way.

SERVINGS - 4

Ingredients

for frying paneer:

  • 20 cubes paneer
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp curd
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 3 tbsp corn flour
  • oil, for frying

for sauce:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • pinch hing
  • 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 chilli, finely chopped
  • 2 dried red chilli, broken
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp chaat masala
  • few curry leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate the paneer

    2. In a mixing bowl, coat paneer cubes with chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, curd, ginger-garlic paste, and cornflour. Mix until evenly coated. Let it rest for 15–20 minutes.

  2. Fry the paneer

    3. Heat oil in a pan over medium flame. Deep fry the marinated paneer pieces until golden and crispy. Set them aside on paper towels to drain excess oil.

  3. Prepare the sauce

    4. In a new pan, heat 2 tbsp oil. Add ginger, garlic, green and red chillies, and sauté for a minute. Add tomato ketchup, chilli powder, salt, and chaat masala. Stir until the sauce becomes glossy.

  4. Combine

    5. Toss in the fried paneer and curry leaves. Mix well until every piece is nicely coated in the spicy sauce.

  5. Serve

    6. Serve hot as a snack or starter with a squeeze of lemon or mint chutney on the side.
